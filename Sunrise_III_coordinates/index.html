Sunrise III:
Co-observations

The balloon-borne solar observatory Sunrise III is a mission that will look at the Sun from the stratosphere using a 1-meter telescope, three scientific instruments, and an image stabilization system.

    News

    Sunrise III was cleared for launch. The launch is expected to be on June 1st at 10 UT
